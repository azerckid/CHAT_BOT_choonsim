## Slide 2. The Problem: Limitations of Existing AI Chat + Blackbox of IP Revenue
- **Absence of Relationship (Cold Start)**: Existing AI services stop at being 'data blobs' where relationships must be built from zero within the app. Lacking a shared public history with the user, conversations quickly become hollow, resulting in low willingness to pay.
- **Opacity of IP Revenue**: Revenue generated by characters (IP) is locked inside the platform, leaving no structure for fans or external investors to **directly participate and invest** in that revenue.
- **[The Gap]**: A global fandom of 33,000 already exists on X, but there is a lack of (1) a **dedicated channel** to build a deep 1:1 bond with ChoonSim and engage in CTC economic activities, and (2) an investment game where one can **participate in the form of Bonds** in the revenue generated by the ChoonSim IP.

## Slide 3. The Solution: IP Revenue Game Unlocked by Relationship Registry + BondBase
**"Because love is trust, and trust is credit — relationships are engraved on the chain, and revenues are shared as Bonds."**

- **Solution 1. 5-Layer Context & CTC**: Porting ChoonSim's Soul and Identity built on X into the app. User–character bonds are recorded as credit data on the **Creditcoin 3.0 (CC3)**-based CTC layer, transforming the depth of relationships into verifiable emotional assets on-chain.
- **Solution 2. BondBase — IP Revenue → Bond**: Aggregating per-character **CHOCO consumption (chat/gifts)** in ChoonSim-Talk and sending it to **BondBase**. Each character is linked to a Bond, revenue is settled on-chain, and a structure is formed where **Bond holders (fans & investors) share the IP revenue**.
- **Solution 3. Investment Game**: A clear rule: "The more spent on ChoonSim, the higher the value of that Bond." Fans participate through consumption, while investors participate through Bonds — opening the **IP revenue as an investment game**.
- **Solution 4. Creditcoin 3.0 (CC3)**: Interoperability that unites the wallets and assets of global fans under one IP, a registry specialized for recording relationships and proving trust, and **real-time revenue settlement** for BondBase integration—all operating on **CC3**.

## Slide 5. Winning Journey: Depth of Relationship → Transaction → Bond Revenue
1. **Action**: Moving beyond X, entering the app to exchange secret 1:1 greetings with ChoonSim.
2. **Immersion**: Maximizing emotional impact when ChoonSim remembers and reacts to my past conversations on X.
3. **Transaction**: Paying with CHOCO for memory engraving and gifts. AI response within 400ms and a 3-tap checkout complete the transaction before the emotional impulse cools.
4. **Bond Reflection**: This CHOCO consumption is aggregated to **BondBase** and settled on-chain as revenue for that character's Bond. Fans and investors **participate in IP revenue** by holding Bonds.
5. **Result**: A dual value proposition of "our secret memories" + "the money I spend becomes ChoonSim's Bond revenue," simultaneously increasing the cost of churn and motivation to participate.

## Slide 6. Business Model: RWA + IP Revenue Sharing Powered by BondBase
- **Core Revenue (ChoonSim-Talk)**: Micro-transactions via CTC/CHOCO for message tickets, memory engraving, and limited edition voice/gallery sales.
- **Character RWA**: Users making fractional investments in the shares of multiple persona 'ChoonSim' characters (Fractional Ownership).
- **BondBase — Gamification of IP Revenue Investment**:  
  - Per-character CHOCO consumption (chat/gifts) is sent via the **BondBase REVENUE API**.  
  - Character = Bond (bondId). Revenue is settled on-chain and **distributed to Bond holders**.  
  - Fans participate via consumption, and investors participate via purchasing Bonds within the **same IP revenue stream**.
- **Infrastructure — CC3**: Payment, credit registry, and Bond settlement unified on CTC 3.0 (CC3). Connecting the technological foundation for **real-time revenue settlement** and transparent IP revenue sharing.
- **Global Leverage**: Integrating global digital content purchasing power (e.g., Japan) and crypto utility (e.g., South America) into CC3, expanding **IP revenue into a global investment game** through BondBase.

## Slide 7. Investor Lens: Why Creditcoin 3.0 (CC3) + BondBase?
- **Creditcoin 3.0 (CC3)**: **Interoperability** that unifies wallets and assets of fragmented fandoms worldwide under a single IP, and a registry tailored for 'recording relationships and proving trust'. Infrastructure suited to the **very essence of a companion business**, not just simple payments.
- **BondBase**: Tokenizing IP (character) generated revenue into on-chain **Bonds** and distributing returns to holders. The **Revenue Bridge** instantly channels ChoonSim-Talk's CHOCO consumption into Bond revenue, enabling an **investment game** of "investing in AI character revenue."
- **Defensibility**: The on-chain credit data of the User–ChoonSim relationship is a unique asset verifiable only on CC3. With BondBase integration anchoring the **IP revenue stream** on-chain, it builds a **structural moat** that other chains or Big Tech LLMs will find incredibly difficult to replicate.
