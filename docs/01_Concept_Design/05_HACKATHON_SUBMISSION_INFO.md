## Details (본문)

ChoonSim-Talk: Raise-to-Earn AI Companion on Creditcoin 3.0

ChoonSim-Talk is a relationship-sustaining AI companion built on a real IP with 33,000+ global fans on X (Twitter). Unlike generic AI chatbots that start from zero, ChoonSim carries a living history — remembering shared moments, past conversations, and emotional context across platforms.

The Problem:
Existing AI services lack shared history with users, making conversations hollow and engagement shallow. Meanwhile, revenue generated by character IPs stays locked inside platforms — fans and investors have no way to participate.

Our Solution:
We combine AI companionship with on-chain investment mechanics to create the first "Raise-to-Earn" model. ChoonSim's personality and emotional identity are ported from X into the app, creating deep 1:1 bonds recorded as on-chain credit data on Creditcoin 3.0. Every CHOCO spent (chat, gifts, memory engraving) is aggregated per character and sent to BondBase, where it becomes on-chain Bond revenue shared with Bond holders. Like a character-raising game — the more you nurture ChoonSim, the higher the Bond value. Fans play to grow, investors buy to earn.

Tech Stack:
Creditcoin 3.0 (CC3) for payments, credit registry, and Bond settlement. BondBase for on-chain revenue tokenization. React Router v7 + Vercel Edge for global PWA deployment. Multi-layer AI context engine with 400ms response time.

Traction:
33,000+ global fans on X (Japan, LATAM, Middle East, SEA). 20+ new fans daily engaging 1:1 with AI ChoonSim. CHOCO to BondBase REVENUE pipeline fully operational.

What's Next (6-18 months):
Credit-based AI Agent for on-chain economic operations. Yield-bearing AI character marketplace on CC3. Open builder ecosystem via MCP, x402, and BondBase Revenue Bridge.
